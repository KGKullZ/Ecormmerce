About Us

K and K Appliance Services was founded in 2010. In addition to repairing and servicing all famous brands of household and commercial appliances, we supply and install air conditioning and heat pump water heating systems. Among the many services we offer at K and K are appliance repairs, air conditioning sales, and installation. It is our goal to provide our customers with professional quality service. The fact that we do not only focus on one specific brand allows us to provide our customers with the best product at the best price, thereby ensuring maximum value for money.
